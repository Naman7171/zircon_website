About Us

Welcome to Zircon Club, PCE Jaipur's premier hub for Robotics and Technology enthusiasts. Established in 2008, we are a dynamic community fostering innovation and practical skills in Robotics, IoT, and more. Our vibrant activities include expert-led workshops, industry seminars, and exciting competitions. Explore your technical prowess through our annual college TechFest, Aadhar, where knowledge meets creativity. With a vision to be a regional tech leader, we provide a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and innovate. Join us in shaping the future of technology – where Zircon Club is not just a club; it's a journey of exploration and excellence.
